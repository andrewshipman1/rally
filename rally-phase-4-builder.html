phase 4 · trip builder

The trip page IS the builder.

No separate flow. No multi-step wizard. You hit "+" on the dashboard and you're dropped straight into a half-built trip page with placeholder fields you fill inline. Same chassis. Same wordmark. Same sticker treatment. Same countdown card — just empty until you set a date. Same crew row — just one avatar (you) and a tap-to-add button. The trip is alive from second one; you're not "creating" it, you're filling in a thing that already exists.

Why this and not a wizard

The wizard kills the brand. A multi-step form is the most calendar-app-coded surface possible. Rally's whole pitch is "your trip is alive the moment you start it" — a wizard is the opposite of that. It's a vestibule.

Four fields is not a form. Name, when, where, crew. That's it. Inline placeholders + tap-to-edit handles all of it without ceremony.

The empty state IS the marketing. A blank rally page that already has a wiggling sticker, a marquee, a countdown card waiting for a date, and a crew row waiting for faces — that's the screenshot you'd send a friend. A wizard's first step is not.

What you're seeing below

Light + dark mode side by side, both in pure sketch state (nothing filled in). Marquee scaffolding text reads "tap to name · set the dates · invite the crew" instead of trip facts. Sticker says "new rally ✨" instead of a countdown. Title is a giant Shrikhand placeholder with a blinking caret. The countdown card sits there with a "?" waiting for dates. The CTA is dashed-out and disabled until the trip has a name + dates + at least one other person.

One handwritten hint in Caveat sits below the title field nudging you toward what to do first. It disappears once you start typing.

Builder → live transition (the moment of truth)

The trigger: name + at least one date + at least one other person added. That's the bar to flip from sketch to live. CTA un-disables and changes copy from "add the basics first" to "send it to the group 🚀".

What changes visually: the dashed field borders go solid · the title placeholder color goes full opacity (or shows the typed name) · the countdown "??" snaps to a real number with a quick count-down animation · the live-row swaps from "draft · only you can see this" to "trip is live" · the sticker swaps from "new rally ✨" to a countdown sticker like "57 days 💍" · the marquee swaps from scaffolding tasks to trip facts.

What does NOT change: the chassis. Same wordmark, same layout, same outlines, same shadows, same animations. The trip page didn't become a trip page — it always was one. You just turned the lights on.
