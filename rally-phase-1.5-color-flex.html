Rally — Phase 1.5: same chassis, three palettes.

Direction B (the winner) shown in three color modes across three different fake trips. The point: the chassis is the brand. Color is the variable. Same fonts, same outlines, same drop shadows, same sticker treatment, same voice — only the palette rotates.

The kernel (this never changes)

Type stack: Shrikhand display + Caveat handwritten + DM Sans body. Outlines: 2–3px hard black on everything that matters. Drop shadows: 4–6px solid offset (no blur). Stickers: rotated, outlined, "made by a friend" energy. Voice: friend-of-a-friend in the group chat, microcopy too unserious not too clean.

The variable: color palette. Three swatches per theme — a background, an accent, a sticker fill — plus an ink color. That's the entire flex. 12 themes = 12 palettes inside one chassis.
