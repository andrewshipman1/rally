phase 9 · crew · g13

the crew subsurface

Who's coming, who's thinking, who's out. A simple expanded view of the guest list, reachable from the trip page. Anyone on the trip can open it — it's just visibility, not control. For v0 this is read-only: no kicks, no nudges, no role management. Just a clean at-a-glance of where everyone stands.

what's here

Route: /trip/{slug}/crew — reachable from the RSVP row on the trip page

Audience: anyone who can see the trip (host and invitees, regardless of their own RSVP state)

V0 scope locked:

  • read-only guest list, grouped by in / holding / out
  • host gets a 👑 marker, "you" gets a tag
  • summary strip at top (3 counts, no trending)
  • "out" people stay visible but dimmed + strikethrough
  • +1s show as sub-text under the inviter's row (not separate rows)

Deferred: nudging, kicking, role changes, last-active timestamps, per-person notes, contact reveal, co-host promotion. Add later once the basics feel right.
