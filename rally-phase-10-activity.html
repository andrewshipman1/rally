phase 10 · buzz · g14

the activity feed

A single reverse-chron feed that mixes system events (rsvps, votes, lock-ins, new items) with short chat posts. Compose box at the top, newest stuff directly below it. Reactions on every row — taps, not replies. Everyone on the trip sees it, regardless of RSVP state.

what's here

Route: /trip/{slug}/buzz — tab in the bottom nav

Audience: everyone on the trip (in, holding, out — all see the same feed)

V0 scope locked:

  • compose at top: short text posts, Caveat placeholder ("what's the word?")
  • feed below: reverse-chron, newest pinned to the compose box so posting feels immediate
  • two row types: event (flat text, sticker-colored icon) and post (chat bubble, "mine" flips sides + accent color)
  • reactions (👍 🔥 😂 🎿 etc.) on any row — tap to add, tap again to remove. One pill cluster under each row.
  • day dividers (today, yesterday, monday) to keep reverse-chron grouped without breaking the flow

Deferred: threading/replies, @mentions, media uploads, read receipts, muting, edit/delete of posts, push notification preferences. Chat here is intentionally brief — iMessage is still where the real back-and-forth lives.
